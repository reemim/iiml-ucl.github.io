Research Interests

Machine Learning, natural language processing (NLP), cultural alignment for LLMs, and business applications.

About

Reem Masoud is a PhD student in the Department of Electronic and Electrical Engineering at University College London. Her research focuses on cultural alignment in large language models and evaluation methods for multilingual and multimodal systems. She holds an MSc in Control Systems from Imperial College London, where her dissertation explored algorithmic optimization for financial trading. Prior to academia, she worked at Procter & Gamble in operations leadership roles.
