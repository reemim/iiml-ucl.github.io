About

Mohammed Alshaalan is a PhD candidate at the Department of Electronic and Electrical Engineering at University College London (UCL), focusing on improving Vision Transformers within machine learning. He earned his Masters in Applied Data Analytics from Boston University and a Bachelor's in Software Engineering from King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM). With seven years of professional experience at Saudi Aramco, Mohammed brings a blend of practical and academic expertise to his research at UCL.
